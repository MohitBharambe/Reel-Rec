Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reel Rec?

Reel Rec is a Movie Recommendation AI which recommends movies to its users based on their preferences.

How to use it?

That's a straightforward process! Simply navigate to the recommendations page and conduct a search for a movie you're familiar with. You'll receive recommendations for similar movies based on your search.

What is a Recommendation AI?

A Recommendation AI or a recommendation system is an artificial intelligence algorithm, that uses Big Data to suggest or recommend additional products to users. these recommendations can be based on various criterias, including search history, demographic information, and other factors. Popular Tech Giants like Netflix and Amazon use recommendation systems to enhance user experience by providing personalized content suggestions based on their viewing or purchasing history.

Why the name 'Reel Rec'?

The AI is named Reel Rec because it encapsulates the core idea and purpose of the system. 'Reel' is a term commonly associated with movies and cinema, and 'Rec' is a short form of 'recommendation.' The combination of these words signifies that the AI is designed to recommend movies to users.

How Accurate are the Recommendations?

The Recommendations are entirely determined by the user's movie preferences. When a user searches for Action Movies, Reel Rec will suggest similar Action Movies, and the same principle applies to other genres as well.

Why was I unable to find the movie I was searching for?

We acknowledge that you were unable to find the movie you were searching for. However, please note that our database currently boasts a collection of over 10,000 movies. We are continuously updating our database, so the movie you're looking for may be available soon.

Does Reel Rec recommends TV Shows?

No, at the moment, Reel Rec only includes movies. However, we have plans to expand our content to include TV shows and documentaries in the future.

How do i report a problem?
